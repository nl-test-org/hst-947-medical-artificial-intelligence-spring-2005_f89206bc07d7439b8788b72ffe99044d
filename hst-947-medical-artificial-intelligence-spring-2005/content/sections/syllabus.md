Course Meeting Times
--------------------

Lectures: 2 sessions / week, 1.5 hours / session

Recitations: 1 session / week, 1 hour / session

Discussions: 1 session / week, 1 hour / session

Course Description
------------------

HST.947Â meets with and builds upon the lectures and recitations of 6.034: Artificial Intelligence. Students are responsible for completing all homework assignments in 6.034 as well as additional problems and a term paper specifically assigned in HST.947.
